I will be starting as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the National University of Singapore (NUS) in Jan 2021. I am actively seeking students who are interested in the mathematics of data science and/or optimization, and wish to work with me. Please write to my email address and apply through this website.

I am Research Scientist in the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC). I am also an Adjunct Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the National University of Singapore (NUS) .

I am broadly interested in mathematical aspects of data science and its interplay with optimization. I am specifically interested in understanding the role that convex optimization (and convexity in general) plays in data processing, as well as the limits that are involved in terms of statistical inference and computation. My most recent projects revolve the around the question of estimating a convex body from data-points.

I obtained my PhD in Applied and Computational Mathematics (ACM) from the Department of Computing and Mathematical Sciences (CMS) at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) in 2018. My advisor is Venkat Chandrasekaran. Before that, I was as a Research Engineer at the Institute for Infocomm Research from 2011 to 2012 under the supervision of Tony Quek, and also in collaboration with Marios Kountouris. I obtained my BA in Mathematics from the University of Cambridge (UK) in 2011.
